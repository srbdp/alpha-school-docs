---
title: "Contact Information"
api: "GET /contact/overview"
---

Contact methods and communication channels for Alpha School inquiries.

## Response

<ResponseField name="primary_contact_method" type="string">
  Main contact channel for general inquiries.

  **Example:** `"Web form at alpha.school/learn-more"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="general_emails" type="array">
  Publicly available purpose-specific email addresses.

  **Example:** `["registrar@alpha.school", "admissions@alpha.school"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="press_email" type="string">
  Media inquiry contact.

  **Example:** `"press@2hourlearning.com"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="headquarters_address" type="string">
  Corporate headquarters (same as Austin PK-8 campus).

  **Example:** `"1201 Spyglass Dr, Austin, TX 78746"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="phone_availability" type="string">
  General phone contact status.

  **Example:** `"Not publicly available (web form-first strategy)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="social_media" type="object">
  Official social media handles.

  **Example:** `{"instagram": "@alphaschool_2hrlearning", "twitter": "@AlphaSchoolATX", "facebook": "@AlphaSchoolAustin", "youtube": "@thealphaschool", "linkedin": "alphaschools"}`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="careers_portal" type="string">
  Employment/job applications (external platform).

  **Example:** `"https://www.crossover.com/clients/alpha"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="contact_form" type="string">
  General inquiry form URL.

  **Example:** `"https://alpha.school/learn-more/"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="events_page" type="string">
  Upcoming info sessions and events.

  **Example:** `"https://alpha.school/programs-events/"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="faq_page" type="string">
  Self-service frequently asked questions.

  **Example:** `"https://alpha.school/faq/"`
</ResponseField>
