---
title: "Grade Levels"
api: "GET /curriculum/grade-levels"
---

Alpha School's PreK-12 capability-based level system.

## Response

<ResponseField name="grade_range" type="string">
  Overall grade levels served across Alpha School locations.

  **Example:** `"PreK-12 (varies by location)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="grade_range_variability" type="boolean">
  Whether grade ranges differ by campus location.

  **Example:** `true`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="level_basis" type="string">
  How students are grouped and advance through curriculum.

  **Example:** `"Capability-based levels (mastery-driven) vs. traditional age-based grades"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="prek_8_structure" type="string">
  Learning approach for younger students.

  **Example:** `"Structured curriculum with prescribed workshops and guided academic progression"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="high_school_structure" type="string">
  Learning approach for older students.

  **Example:** `"Self-directed super passion projects with dedicated mentorship and greater autonomy"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="standards_k8" type="string">
  Curriculum standards for elementary/middle school.

  **Example:** `"Common Core (K-8)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="standards_high_school" type="string">
  Curriculum standards for high school.

  **Example:** `"Advanced Placement (AP Calculus, Statistics, English, Sciences)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="progression_basis" type="string">
  How students advance between levels.

  **Example:** `"Demonstrated mastery (90%+ accuracy), not age or time"`
</ResponseField>
