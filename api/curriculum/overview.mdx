---
title: "Curriculum Overview"
api: "GET /curriculum/overview"
---

Alpha School's mastery-based K-12 curriculum philosophy and structure.

## Response

<ResponseField name="curriculum_philosophy" type="string">
  Core pedagogical approach emphasizing proficiency over time-based advancement.

  **Example:** `"Mastery-based learning with personalized pacing and spaced repetition"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="mastery_threshold" type="string">
  Proficiency requirement before advancement (note source discrepancy).

  **Example:** `"Over 90% accuracy (alpha.school) or 80% (secondary sources)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="core_subjects" type="array">
  Academic subjects taught in morning block.

  **Example:** `["Math", "English/Language Arts", "Science", "Social Studies", "Reading", "Writing", "Foreign Language"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="core_subjects_duration" type="string">
  Daily time allocation for academic subjects.

  **Example:** `"2 hours (morning block with Pomodoro 25-minute sessions)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="life_skills_count" type="number">
  Number of life skills emphasized across curriculum.

  **Example:** `24`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="life_skills_duration" type="string">
  Daily time allocation for life skills workshops.

  **Example:** `"~4 hours (afternoon, screen-free, hands-on)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="standards_alignment" type="object">
  Curriculum standards followed by grade level.

  **Example:** `{"K-8": "Common Core", "High School": "Advanced Placement"}`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="delivery_model" type="string">
  Instructional delivery approach.

  **Example:** `"AI-powered personalized instruction via third-party and proprietary platforms, curated by central 'Dash' system"`
</ResponseField>
