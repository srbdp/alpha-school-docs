---
title: "Curriculum Subjects"
api: "GET /curriculum/subjects"
---

Academic subjects taught at Alpha School and their delivery platforms.

## Response

<ResponseField name="core_subjects" type="array">
  Seven academic subjects taught during morning block.

  **Example:** `["Math", "English/Language Arts", "Science", "History/Social Studies", "Reading", "Writing", "Foreign Language"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="delivery_duration" type="string">
  Daily time allocation for all academic subjects.

  **Example:** `"2 hours"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="delivery_method" type="string">
  Instructional delivery approach.

  **Example:** `"AI-powered adaptive platforms with personalized instruction"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="central_platform" type="string">
  Platform orchestrating curriculum pathway.

  **Example:** `"Dash"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="proprietary_platforms" type="array">
  Alpha School-developed applications.

  **Example:** `["Alpha Math", "AlphaRead", "AlphaWrite", "Teach Tales", "Fluency Coach"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="third_party_platforms" type="array">
  External platforms integrated into curriculum.

  **Example:** `["Khan Academy", "Grammarly", "Math Academy", "Synthesis Tutor", "Amira", "Lalio"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="standards_k8" type="string">
  K-8 curriculum standards alignment.

  **Example:** `"Common Core"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="standards_high_school" type="string">
  High school curriculum standards alignment.

  **Example:** `"Advanced Placement (AP Calculus, Statistics, English Language, English Literature, Biology, Chemistry, Physics)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="mastery_threshold" type="string">
  Required proficiency level before advancement.

  **Example:** `"90%+ accuracy (alpha.school) or 80%+ (secondary sources)"`
</ResponseField>
