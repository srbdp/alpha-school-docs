---
title: "Tuition and Financial Aid"
api: "GET /enrollment/tuition"
---

Alpha School's location-based tuition pricing and financial aid options.

## Response

<ResponseField name="tuition_range" type="string">
  Minimum and maximum annual tuition across all Alpha locations.

  **Example:** `"$45,000 - $75,000/year"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="location_pricing" type="object">
  Tuition amounts by specific campus location.

  **Example:** `{"san_francisco": "$75,000", "austin": "$40,000", "gt_school": "$25,000", "brownsville": "$10,000", "micro_schools": "~$15,000"}`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="all_inclusive_model" type="boolean">
  Whether tuition covers all student activities and costs.

  **Example:** `true`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="application_fee" type="string">
  Non-refundable fee required with application.

  **Example:** `"$100 per student"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="enrollment_deposit" type="string">
  Deposit amount to secure enrollment spot (applies to tuition).

  **Example:** `"$1,000 per student"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="sibling_discount" type="string">
  Tuition reduction for multiple enrolled children.

  **Example:** `"5% for second child and beyond"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="financial_aid_platform" type="string">
  Third-party system for aid applications.

  **Example:** `"Clarity"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="voucher_compatibility" type="boolean">
  Whether school accepts state vouchers.

  **Example:** `true`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="typical_voucher_amount" type="string">
  Common voucher value in eligible states.

  **Example:** `"~$12,000/year"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="included_in_tuition" type="array">
  What all-inclusive tuition covers.

  **Example:** `["field trips", "supplies", "enrichment programs", "afternoon workshops", "sports", "arts", "technology", "MAP assessments"]`
</ResponseField>
