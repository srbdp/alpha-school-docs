---
title: "Expansion Plans"
api: "GET /locations/expansion"
---

Alpha School's announced expansion plans and strategic growth initiatives.

## Response

<ResponseField name="confirmed_fall_2026_openings" type="array">
  Campuses with announced Fall 2026 opening dates.

  **Example:** `["The Woodlands, TX", "Dorado, Puerto Rico", "Palo Alto, CA", "Santa Monica, CA", "Chicago, IL"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="exploratory_targets" type="array">
  Cities/regions under exploration without confirmed dates.

  **Example:** `["Aspen, CO", "Houston, TX", "Orlando, FL", "Phoenix, AZ", "East Bay, CA"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="strategic_partnerships" type="object">
  Key partnerships driving expansion.

  **Example:** `{"Guidepost Montessori": "July 2025", "Higher Ground Education": "July 2025"}`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="texas_sports_academy_locations" type="number">
  Sport-specific campus openings (Jan + Fall 2026).

  **Example:** `4`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="timeline_acceleration" type="string">
  Growth timeline improvement through partnerships.

  **Example:** `"~2 years faster"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="expansion_mechanism" type="array">
  Methods for entering new markets.

  **Example:** `["partnerships", "acquisitions", "organic_development"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="geographic_focus" type="string">
  Primary market characteristics.

  **Example:** `"major metros and affluent suburbs"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="partnership_impact" type="string">
  Fast-tracked campus opening timeline.

  **Example:** `"10 new K-8 campuses by December 2025"`
</ResponseField>
