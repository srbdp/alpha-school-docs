---
title: "Locations Overview"
api: "GET /locations/overview"
---

Alpha School's campus network across 8 U.S. states.

## Response

<ResponseField name="total_campuses_operating" type="number">
  Currently operating Alpha School locations as of January 2026.

  **Example:** `17`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="states_served" type="number">
  Number of U.S. states with Alpha campuses.

  **Example:** `8`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="headquarters_location" type="string">
  Corporate headquarters city and state.

  **Example:** `"Austin, Texas"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="founding_campus_year" type="number">
  Year original Alpha School campus was established.

  **Example:** `2016`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="grade_levels_range" type="string">
  Span of grade levels served across network.

  **Example:** `"PreK-12 (varies by campus)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="tuition_range" type="string">
  Minimum and maximum annual tuition across all locations.

  **Example:** `"$10,000 - $75,000/year"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="standard_operating_hours" type="string">
  Universal campus daily schedule.

  **Example:** `"8:15 AM - 4:00 PM"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="outdoor_play_minimum" type="string">
  Required daily outdoor time across all campuses.

  **Example:** `"60-90 minutes"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="student_guide_ratio" type="string">
  Staffing ratio maintained across network.

  **Example:** `"5:1"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="expansion_campuses_planned" type="number">
  Announced Fall 2026 new locations.

  **Example:** `5`
</ResponseField>
