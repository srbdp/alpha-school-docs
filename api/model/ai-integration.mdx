---
title: "AI Integration"
api: "GET /model/ai-integration"
---

Alpha School's AI platforms and adaptive learning technology.

## Response

<ResponseField name="platform_names" type="array">
  Primary AI platforms used for instruction.

  **Example:** `["Timeback", "Dash", "Incept"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="primary_function" type="string">
  Core AI role in student learning.

  **Example:** `"Tutor and coach providing personalized instruction"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="instructional_approach" type="string">
  AI delivery method (explicitly NOT chatbots).

  **Example:** `"Personalized one-on-one instruction through adaptive AI (NOT chatbots like ChatGPT)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="knowledge_modeling" type="string">
  How AI tracks and represents student understanding.

  **Example:** `"Creates 'knowledge graph' of what each student knows, combined with interests"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="adaptivity_features" type="array">
  Real-time adjustment mechanisms.

  **Example:** `["Real-time performance monitoring", "Lesson difficulty adjustment", "Zone of proximal development targeting", "Mastery-based progression (90%+)", "Gap identification and targeted practice"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="technology_stack" type="string">
  Platform development approach.

  **Example:** `"Ever-evolving mix of adaptive learning apps both built and bought"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="third_party_tools" type="array">
  External platforms integrated into Alpha's AI system.

  **Example:** `["IXL", "Khan Academy", "Grammarly", "Newsela"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="custom_tools" type="array">
  Alpha-built platforms and features.

  **Example:** `["Alpha Math", "Vision models for work pattern analysis", "AI-powered curriculum recommendation", "Personalized lesson planning systems"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="platform_owner" type="string">
  Technology development entity.

  **Example:** `"Trilogy Software (division of Trilogy Enterprises, Joe Liemandt)"`
</ResponseField>
