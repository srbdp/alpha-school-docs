---
title: "Daily Schedule"
api: "GET /model/daily-schedule"
---

Alpha School's daily schedule structure for academics and life skills.

## Response

<ResponseField name="day_structure" type="string">
  Overall daily organization pattern.

  **Example:** `"Morning academics (2 hrs) → Lunch → Afternoon life skills (2.5-4 hrs)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="morning_duration" type="string">
  Academic block length.

  **Example:** `"2 hours"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="morning_start_ritual" type="string">
  Day-opening activity.

  **Example:** `"Limitless Launch goal-setting session"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="lunch_transition" type="string">
  Break between morning and afternoon.

  **Example:** `"Lunch break after 10:00 AM academic completion"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="afternoon_duration" type="string">
  Life skills block length range.

  **Example:** `"2.5-4 hours"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="core_subjects_timing" type="string">
  When academics occur.

  **Example:** `"Morning block, typically 8:00-10:00 AM"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="life_skills_timing" type="string">
  When life skills occur.

  **Example:** `"Afternoon, post-lunch"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="life_skills_count" type="number">
  Number of life skills taught.

  **Example:** `24`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="real_world_projects" type="array">
  Examples of student-run projects.

  **Example:** `["Airbnb operations", "Food trucks", "Athletic training", "Public speaking", "Outdoor expeditions"]`
</ResponseField>
