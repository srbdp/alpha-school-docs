---
title: "Educational Philosophy"
api: "GET /model/educational-philosophy"
---

Alpha School's educational philosophy and learning science principles.

## Response

<ResponseField name="foundational_principles" type="array">
  Core learning science theories cited.

  **Example:** `["Bloom's 2 Sigma", "Zone of Proximal Development", "Cognitive Load Theory", "Active Learning Principles"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="core_commitments" type="array">
  Three explicit promises to parents.

  **Example:** `["Love school more than vacation", "Master academics in top 1% in 2 hours/day", "High standards, high support"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="pedagogical_approach" type="string">
  Primary teaching methodology.

  **Example:** `"Mastery-based personalized learning"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="mastery_threshold" type="number">
  Proficiency requirement before advancement.

  **Example:** `90`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="learning_efficiency_claim" type="string">
  Alpha's characterization of traditional education.

  **Example:** `"Traditional six-hour classroom days inefficient, lecture retention as low as 5%"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="student_experience_philosophy" type="string">
  Core belief about school experience.

  **Example:** `"Kids should love school"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="instructional_model" type="string">
  Teaching structure approach.

  **Example:** `"AI-powered one-on-one instruction vs. traditional one-teacher-to-many"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="time_philosophy" type="string">
  Learning progression approach.

  **Example:** `"Mastery-based vs. time-based system"`
</ResponseField>
