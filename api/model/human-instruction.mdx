Role of human guides in Alpha School's learning model.

## Response

<ResponseField name="role_title" type="string">
  Job title for Alpha School educators.

  **Example:** `"Guide"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="instructional_responsibility" type="string">
  Academic teaching involvement.

  **Example:** `"Zero academic instruction"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="primary_focus" type="array">
  Core responsibilities.

  **Example:** `["Motivation", "Mentorship", "Emotional support"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="one_on_one_time" type="number">
  Weekly individual meeting duration per student (minutes).

  **Example:** `30`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="monitoring_role" type="string">
  How guides supervise AI learning sessions.

  **Example:** `"Monitor progress, encourage students, use AI data for interventions"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="life_skills_role" type="string">
  Afternoon workshop facilitation.

  **Example:** `"Lead afternoon life skills workshops"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="compensation_minimum" type="number">
  Minimum annual salary (USD).

  **Example:** `100000`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="compensation_context" type="string">
  Comparison to traditional teacher pay.

  **Example:** `"Roughly double average teacher pay in comparable markets"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="recruitment_strategy" type="string">
  Talent sourcing approach.

  **Example:** `"Recruit talented professionals outside traditional education"`
</ResponseField>
