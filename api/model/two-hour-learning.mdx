---
title: "Two-Hour Learning Model"
api: "GET /model/two-hour-learning"
---

Alpha School's Two-Hour Learning Model for morning academics.

## Response

<ResponseField name="academic_duration" type="number">
  Length of morning academic block in minutes.

  **Example:** `120`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="session_structure" type="string">
  Pomodoro technique implementation.

  **Example:** `"25-minute focused blocks with breaks between subjects"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="subjects_covered" type="array">
  Core academic subjects in the 2-hour block.

  **Example:** `["Math", "English", "Science", "Social Studies"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="instructional_model" type="string">
  AI-powered delivery method.

  **Example:** `"AI-powered one-on-one personalized instruction"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="progression_standard" type="string">
  Mastery-based advancement requirement.

  **Example:** `"90%+ accuracy before advancing"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="mastery_claim" type="string">
  Time to complete grade-level material (Alpha's claim).

  **Example:** `"20-30 hours per subject per grade level"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="efficiency_comparison" type="string">
  Alpha's comparison with traditional schooling.

  **Example:** `"20-30 hours vs. 200 hours in traditional schooling (10-15% of time)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="learning_speed_claim" type="string">
  Reported growth rate on standardized tests.

  **Example:** `"2.6x growth on MAP tests (self-reported), top performers 3.6x-6.5x"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="afternoon_component" type="string">
  What happens after academics.

  **Example:** `"2.5-4 hours of life skills workshops and activities"`
</ResponseField>
