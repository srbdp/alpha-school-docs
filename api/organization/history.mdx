Alpha School's evolution from microschool to national AI-powered education network.

## Response

<ResponseField name="original_name" type="string">
  School's founding name.

  **Example:** `"Emergent Academy"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="founding_year" type="number">
  Year school was founded.

  **Example:** `2014`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="first_campus_year" type="number">
  Year first formal campus opened.

  **Example:** `2016`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="founding_location" type="string">
  Where school originated.

  **Example:** `"Brian Holtz's house, Austin area"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="initial_student_count" type="number">
  Number of students at founding.

  **Example:** `16`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="founding_inspiration" type="string">
  Catalyst for founding.

  **Example:** `"Daughters found school boring"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="original_model" type="string">
  Early educational approach.

  **Example:** `"Acton Academy microschool branch using adaptive apps"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="ai_investment_year" type="number">
  Year of major AI platform investment.

  **Example:** `2022`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="first_graduating_class" type="number">
  Year of first senior class graduation.

  **Example:** `2025`
</ResponseField>
