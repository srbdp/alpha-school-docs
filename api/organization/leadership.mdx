---
title: "Leadership"
api: "GET /organization/leadership"
---

Alpha School's founder-led governance structure and key leadership.

## Response

<ResponseField name="governance_model" type="string">
  Organizational leadership structure.

  **Example:** `"Founder-led private school"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="founder_count" type="number">
  Number of founders.

  **Example:** `2`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="principal" type="string">
  School principal.

  **Example:** `"Joe Liemandt"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="co_founders" type="array">
  Co-founders.

  **Example:** `["MacKenzie Price", "Andrew Price"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="founding_year" type="number">
  Year organization founded.

  **Example:** `2014`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="key_affiliations" type="array">
  Related organizations.

  **Example:** `["Trilogy Software", "ESW Capital", "2 Hour Learning"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="public_leadership_pages" type="boolean">
  Dedicated leadership page exists.

  **Example:** `false`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="leadership_background" type="string">
  Primary expertise areas.

  **Example:** `"Technology entrepreneurship, education innovation, finance"`
</ResponseField>
