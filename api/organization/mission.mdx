---
title: "Mission, Vision, Values"
api: "GET /organization/mission"
---

Alpha School's driving purpose, long-term vision, and core values.

## Response

<ResponseField name="foundational_belief" type="string">
  Core belief about children's capacity.

  **Example:** `"Children are limitless"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="mission_focus" type="string">
  Primary organizational purpose.

  **Example:** `"Unlocking children's potential through revolutionary education"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="vision_goal" type="string">
  Long-term aspirational product goal.

  **Example:** `"A sub-$1,000 tablet that teaches everything in two hours"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="vision_timeline" type="string">
  Timeframe for democratization vision.

  **Example:** `"Reaching a billion kids within 20 years"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="core_commitments" type="array">
  Three explicit promises to parents.

  **Example:** `["Love School", "Learn 2x in 2hrs", "Learn Life Skills"]`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="learning_philosophy" type="string">
  Educational approach foundation.

  **Example:** `"Mastery-based vs. time-based system (90%+ proficiency required)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="equity_goal" type="string">
  Democratization objective.

  **Example:** `"From $10k-$75k tuition to $1k tablet accessible globally"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="values" type="array">
  Core principles (implicit, inferred).

  **Example:** `["High Standards, High Support", "Mastery Over Time", "Holistic Development", "Teacher Investment"]`
</ResponseField>
