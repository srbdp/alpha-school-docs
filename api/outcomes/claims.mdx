---
title: "Outcomes Claims"
api: "GET /outcomes/claims"
---

Alpha School's self-reported performance claims.

## Response

<ResponseField name="learning_speed_claim" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about student learning pace.

  **Example:** `"Students learn 2x as fast as traditional peers (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="map_growth_claim_average" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about average MAP test growth rate.

  **Example:** `"2.6x growth vs. national norms (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="map_growth_claim_top_performers" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about top performer MAP growth.

  **Example:** `"Up to 6.5x growth for top performers (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="map_percentile_claim" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about school-level MAP performance.

  **Example:** `"School-level results nearly always 99th percentile (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="sat_score_claim_average" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about average SAT scores.

  **Example:** `"1470 average SAT (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="sat_score_claim_senior_median" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about senior class median SAT.

  **Example:** `"1535 senior median SAT, reported as highest in Texas (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="ap_exam_performance_claim" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about AP exam achievement.

  **Example:** `"90% earn 4s or 5s on AP exams (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="college_matriculation_claim" type="string">
  Reported college matriculation for first graduating class.

  **Example:** `"11 of 12 first graduates (91.7%) to four-year universities including Stanford, Vanderbilt, USC, UT Austin"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="student_satisfaction_claim" type="string">
  Alpha School's claim about student satisfaction.

  **Example:** `"96% say they love school, 40-60% prefer school to vacation (Alpha School claim)"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="verification_status" type="string">
  Independent verification status of outcome claims.

  **Example:** `"Self-reported without independent third-party verification from academic institutions or peer-reviewed research"`
</ResponseField>
