---
title: "Outcomes Limitations"
api: "GET /outcomes/limitations"
---

Critical gaps in Alpha School's outcomes documentation.

## Response

<ResponseField name="independent_verification_status" type="string">
  Whether third-party academic audits or peer-reviewed research exist.

  **Example:** `"None - all claims self-reported without independent academic verification"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="peer_reviewed_research_status" type="string">
  Whether academic studies validate 2-Hour Learning effectiveness.

  **Example:** `"None - no published peer-reviewed studies on model effectiveness"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="raw_data_availability" type="string">
  Whether raw MAP scores, RIT scores, percentiles are publicly available.

  **Example:** `"Not available - no raw quantitative data published"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="sample_size_disclosure" type="string">
  Whether cohort sizes for performance claims are documented.

  **Example:** `"Not disclosed - one analyzed cohort had only 5 students"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="demographic_data_availability" type="string">
  Whether outcomes by SES, prior performance, learning differences exist.

  **Example:** `"Not available - no demographic breakdowns published"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="attrition_data_availability" type="string">
  Whether retention rates and departure reasons are public.

  **Example:** `"Not available - no attrition data published"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="longitudinal_data_status" type="string">
  Whether multi-year college/career outcomes tracked.

  **Example:** `"Unavailable - first class graduated 2025, too early for longitudinal data"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="methodology_documentation" type="string">
  Whether growth multiplier calculation methods are published.

  **Example:** `"Not documented - no published methodology for 2.6x, 3.6x, 6.5x calculations"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="control_group_studies" type="string">
  Whether RCTs or matched comparison studies exist.

  **Example:** `"None - no randomized controlled trials or matched comparison groups"`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="regulatory_validation_status" type="string">
  Whether education authorities endorse the model.

  **Example:** `"Not validated - charter rejections cite 'untested' model; no accreditation body endorsement"`
</ResponseField>
